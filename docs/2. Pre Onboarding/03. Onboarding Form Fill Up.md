## Onboarding Form Guide:

### Overview:

The Onboarding Form is the first step in setting up your organisation’s Glific account (instance), which you will use to run your WhatsApp chatbot.

Completing the form takes about 5 - 7 minutes. To make the process smoother:

- Keep the required details and documents ready.
- It is recommended that the organization’s signing authority fill out the form

Once submitted, your Glific account will be created, and the login credentials will be shared by the Glific Team via email within 1 working day. 


### Step 1: Access the form :

You will receive the Onboarding Form link from the Glific Team. Click the link to open the form in your browser



### Step 2: Enter the required details in the form:

- **Organisation Name:** Pre-filled.


- **Registered Organisation Name:** As per official documents (e.g., Certificate of Incorporation, GST Registration, PAN Card, or Bank Statement)



<img width="1000" height="430" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2de23b54-8f13-4ba8-985a-033551109200" />

- **Organisation Legal Address:**  Enter the organisation’s legal address exactly as mentioned in official documents.


- **ChatBot Number:** This is the number you will use to run your chatbot. If unavailable at the time of form submission, enter a dummy number: 999999999.


- **Preferred Billing Frequency:** Monthly, Quarterly, or Annually. For more details on pricing, [click here](https://glific.org/pricing/)


- **Signing Authority Contact Details:** Name, Email & Designation of the authorized signatory – An acknowledgement email will be sent after submitting the form to the signing authority.

<img width="1000" height="471" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/279c8a30-62ac-474d-a726-0cb7493080fc" />


- **Submitter Details:** Name and Email ID of the person filling and submitting the form.


- **Invoice Recipients:** Name, Email & Phone number of the person(s) who should receive invoices. (You can add multiple recipients)
It’s recommended to include the Finance team, Program Head, and main Point of Contact (POC) so that all relevant teams stay informed about billing and payment updates.

<img width="1021" height="431" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f68aa0c9-8b9d-4034-a5b7-6cc6d787f7a7" />

- **Legal Documents to Upload:** Upload any one official document (Certification of Incorporation, GST, or PAN - max file size 100 MB)

<img width="1013" height="390" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c839a285-2532-4c99-b1b3-bff91b3f1f97" />

### Step 3: Review & Submit:

- Click on `Read the Terms and Conditions` to read Platform T&C. You can also find it on our website for future reference.


- The other checkbox `I agree to let the Glific team create a support staff account on my Glific setup` will give us consent to create a Support staff account on your platform once created. 
This is crucial and mandatory, as it will enable us to look & resolve any platform related issues that you face in future.


- Once you have read it, click on `I Agree` on the pop-up to agree with the Terms & Conditions.

<img width="1273" height="699" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5a61a7ee-c0d3-4b06-bf65-586bbefa22c2" />


- Then click on `Save` to save your details. This will only save your details, but it will not submit your form. 

<img width="1061" height="630" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2f859cdf-014d-4327-bb5d-1c0a8a5a6478" />

- After you `save` the form, please review & verify the details entered.

- If you want to edit anything, please click on the `Edit` button to enable editing. Once you are done editing, click on `Save` to save the details and review. 

- Once you have reviewed, please click on `Submit` to submit the form.

- Once submitted, our Glific team will verify the details and setup your account & share with you.



**Please Note**- Your billing date will start from the day you have submitted the form.
